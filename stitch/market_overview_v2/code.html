The Rise of Battery Storage Systems

Current trends indicate that 2024 will see a record integration of utility-scale battery storage, projected to grow by 45%. This shift is stabilizing peak-hour pricing in historically volatile regions like Texas and California, creating new arbitrage opportunities for renewable asset owners.
